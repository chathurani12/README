Cyber Security Workshop



our group recently conducted a Cybersecurity workshop for O/L and A/L students, focusing on security fundamentals and threat prevention. The workshop covered essential topics such as cyber threats, malware, phishing attacks, password security, encryption, and safe internet practices. Students learned about ethical hacking, network security, and the importance of data protection through interactive discussions and real-world case studies. Hands-on activities, including identifying vulnerabilities and implementing security measures, helped them gain practical knowledge. The session aimed to raise awareness about cybersecurity risks, equip students with essential skills to protect themselves online, and inspire them to explore careers in cybersecurity.
